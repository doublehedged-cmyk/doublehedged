FAQn°07

Clear answers, before you start.

Everything a careful buyer should know — asked and answered plainly.

Is Double Hedged a tips platform? +

No. Double Hedged is an education platform built around structure, risk, practice, and chart-based learning. We do not provide trading calls, buy/sell recommendations, or advisory messages.

Is this for beginners? +

Yes. The learning path starts with market basics and gradually moves into chart reading, options logic, risk, and execution.

Do you teach options trading? +

Yes. Options are taught through logic — Greeks, theta decay, IV, option chain reading, spreads, and hedging concepts.

Is this specific to Indian markets? +

Yes. Examples and modules focus on Indian traders, Indian timings, and instruments like NIFTY, BANKNIFTY, FINNIFTY, stocks, MCX Gold and MCX Crude.

Do you guarantee profits? +

No. Trading involves market risk. Education can improve process and understanding, but it cannot guarantee returns.

Can I learn with small capital? +

Yes — and the emphasis is on learning, simulation, journaling and risk control before increasing exposure.
